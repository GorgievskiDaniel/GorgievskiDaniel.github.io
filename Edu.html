FINKI
OU "Vasil Glavinov" is a nine-year school whose main activity is the education of students from I - IX grades. The school was founded by the Municipality of Veles on October 23, 1955. The work starts with 22 classes from 1st to 9th grade with 700 students and 30 teachers. The school is verified by Decision No. 10-942/3 of June 8, 1994 of the Ministry of Education and Physical Culture of the Republic of Macedonia. The school is registered in the Central Register with Decision No. 4025563 dated January 26, 2007.

By decision of the assembly of the Municipality of Titov Veles dated September 22, 1965. industrial school UZUS (School for Students in the Economy) "Kole Nedelkovski" was established. Schooling lasted three years and the students were divided into three directions: metal, ceramic and textile. During its existence, the school taught several educational professions such as metal, ceramic, textile, but today the mechanical, electrical, traffic and catering professions are represented.
